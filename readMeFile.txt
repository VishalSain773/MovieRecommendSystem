Movie recommendation based upon the rating given by the user i.e WHAT KIND/GENRE OF MOVIE TO RECOMMEND :-
===================
If rating is 1 or 2 then the movie with same genre will not be recommended anyway.
If rating is 3 then out of any genre movie matched will be recommended.
If rating is 4 then half of the count of genre will be matched and related movies will be recommended.
If rating is 5 then movie with all the same genre/genres will be matched and recommended.



1.) Show the movie list(Show 20 movies randomly).
2.) Select a particular movie from the list and give rating(1-5).
3.) Check the genre of the particular movie.
4.) Decide on the basis of the given rating what to do and what not to do.
